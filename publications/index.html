Publications

realfast publications:

  1. Law et al. (2015) describes a prototype realfast system that searched 200 hours and 200 TB of data for FRBs.
  2. Chatterjee et al. (2017) describes how a realfast prototype made the first interferometric localization and host identification of an FRB (see image below).
  3. Law et al. (2017) present a detailed analysis of radio bursts from FRB 121102.
  4. Law et al. (2018) is the realfast project paper.
  5. Pokorny et al. (2018) describes the high-speed data distribution protocol.
  6. Law et al. (2019) presents a simultaneous fast/slow transient survey targeting SLSN.
  7. Marcote et al. (2020) describes the localization of an FRB by the EVN and includes a deep image from commensal VLA/realfast observations.
